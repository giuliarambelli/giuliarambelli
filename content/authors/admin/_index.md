I am a PhD candidate in Linguistic studies at the University of Pisa in cotutelle with the Aix-Marseille University (AMU), under the joint supervision of [Alessandro Lenci](https://colinglab.fileli.unipi.it/people/rambelli/https://people.unipi.it/alessandro_lenci/) and [Philippe Blache](https://colinglab.fileli.unipi.it/people/rambelli/http://www2.lpl-aix.fr/~blache/). I am a member of the [Computational Liguistics Laboratory ](https://colinglab.fileli.unipi.it/people/rambelli/http://colinglab.humnet.unipi.it/)(University of Pisa) and of the [Laboratoire Parole et Language ](https://colinglab.fileli.unipi.it/people/rambelli/http://www.lpl-aix.fr/)(AMU). I am also enrolled in the [Institute of Language, Communication, and the Brain ](https://colinglab.fileli.unipi.it/people/rambelli/https://www.ilcb.fr/)(ILCB) PhD Program.

My research field are Lexical and Computational Semantics, and Natural Language Processing. In my research, I investigate the mechanisms underlying natural language comprehension, bringing together the theoretical framework of Construction Grammars, usage-based models as Distributional Semantics and psycholinguistic findings.
